Welcome to Elytheria, the little kingdom with the very large heart! Elytheria is both the Kingdom of Elytheria and a proud crown-state of the Union of the United Republics. Our capital, Boletus, is famous for flower lanes, windmills, family farms, warm markets, mushroom-shaped roofs, and one enormous ancient mushroom that refuses to explain itself.

“The crown is happiest when the market is noisy, the gardens are open, and a new neighbor has found a home.” — Queen Neon
NEW RESIDENTS WANTED! Boletus welcomes gardeners, builders, bakers, millers, artists, librarians, merchants, public servants, and anybody who knows how to fix an old guestbook script. Request your cheerful relocation packet!

What is Elytheria?

  • A historic kingdom whose crown and customs continue within the U.U.R.
  • A peaceful agricultural and market state centered on the capital of Boletus.
  • A home for small workshops, public gardens, windmill communities, and unusual mushrooms.
  • A place where Queen Neon represents the crown while elected councils manage ordinary public business under Union law.
