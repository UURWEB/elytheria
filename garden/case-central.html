SUMMARY

The defacement of the obsolete U.U.R. military information page was not an Astari operation. The person calling themself Operator Sandglass was a child in Central who recovered an old public-web password and pasted imitation recruitment material over an abandoned site.

The child never reached a military network, never contacted the Empire, and recruited no one. The matter has been taken care of.

Instruction to operators: do not contact, praise, threaten, or imitate Sandglass.

The spectacle drew attention toward the broken military page and away from quieter routes. The mistake was useful only after it ended.
