HOW OUR LITTLE KINGDOM WORKS

THE ELYTHERIAN FLAG & GOVERNMENT EMBLEM
The golden sunflower over an orange sunburst is used as both Elytheria’s flag and its government emblem. It appears on royal offices, tourism material, Boletus civic signs, and official crown-state documents.

Elytheria is a historic kingdom and a crown-state within the U.U.R. Its old royal institutions continue beside elected civil government. This arrangement lets Elytherians keep their crown, customs, local titles, and garden ceremonies while sharing Union defense, citizenship, infrastructure, and constitutional protections.

InstitutionWhat it does
Queen NeonCeremonial sovereign, guardian of continuity, royal patron, representative of Elytherian custom.
Mushroom CouncilElected state council responsible for services, planning, markets, roads, local revenue, and public works.
Boletus CommonsCapital assembly and public meeting hall for petitions, resident notices, and civic debate.
Royal Garden OfficeMaintains protected gardens, ceremonial grounds, old paths, and public beauty rules that are taken surprisingly seriously.
Union OfficesCoordinate Elytheria with the wider U.U.R. in defense, citizenship, transport, archives, and shared institutions.

Can a kingdom be a state?

Yes. “Kingdom of Elytheria” describes its historic crown and local constitutional identity. “State of Elytheria” describes its place inside the Union. Elytherians use both names, often in the same sentence and occasionally on the same sign.

The Tourism Bureau cannot settle constitutional arguments. It can provide directions to the Commons, where somebody will happily continue the argument.
