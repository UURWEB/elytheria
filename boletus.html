BOLETUS: CAPITAL OF FLOWERS & FRIENDLY CONFUSION

Queen’s Hall, the Sunwell, and the public garden approach.

Boletus is the royal capital, state seat, chief market, garden center, and unofficial argument capital of Elytheria. The city grew around old farms, winding paths, the Great Boletus Tree, and a gathering place that became the modern Commons.
